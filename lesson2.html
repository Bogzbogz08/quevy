The Effect of Textual Aids on the Understanding of a Text

Textual aids are tools or instruments that come in different forms and can make texts easier to understand.

The following are examples of textual aids and their purpose

  1. Titles - provides the reader a certain idea what the topic of a text is.
  2. Illustrations - a visual interpretation of a topic or idea used to clearly explain details or information. A graphic organizer can be used as an Illustration like the flowchart which depicts the several steps of a process.
  3. Graphs - a graphical representation of numerical data that uses symbols such as bar, on a bar chart, line on a line chart, or slices in a pie chart.
  4. Tables - an arrangement of information stored in cells which forms a structure consisting of rows and columns.
