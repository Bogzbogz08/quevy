Elements of the story

Stories are composed of different types of elements that structure the storyline.

These are the elements of the story;

Characters are living beings that perform every action in the story with the protagonist being the center of attention and the one who leads the story.

The Setting is also an important element of the story, it tells where or when the story took place. Even the weather is a part of the setting!

The Plot is what defines the series of events happening in the story, the conflict depicts the disagreement or dispute between opposing forces. The plot is arranged in a chronological order, from the beginning, where foreshadowing, can be mostly found, it gives a hint on what is about to happen later in the story. The middle, this is where the climax, the highest point of the story, mostly happens. Then onto the end, where the story concludes.
